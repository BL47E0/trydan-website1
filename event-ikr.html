IKR 2025

The ISIE Indian Karting Race (IKR) is a national-level student engineering competition organized by the Imperial Society of Innovative Engineers (ISIE). It provides a platform for aspiring engineers to design, fabricate, and validate a go-kart by applying real-world automotive engineering principles. The competition emphasizes both technical knowledge and practical implementation, bridging the gap between classroom learning and industry-level experience.

Achievements

  • AIR 3 Overall Finish
  • 1st Place - Business Plan
  • Clean Pit Award
  • Inspiring Faculty Award
  • 2nd Place - Cost Report

This event helped us improve our design, teamwork, and racing strategy.
