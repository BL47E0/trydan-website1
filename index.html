About Us

Passion Meets Performance

Team Trydan is the official go-kart club of Nitte Meenakshi Institute of Technology (NMIT), Bengaluru, comprising passionate students from diverse engineering disciplines. The team is dedicated to designing and building high-performance go-karts that combine innovation, precision, and engineering excellence. With a shared drive for motorsport and technology, we aim to create machines that deliver speed, safety, and an unforgettable racing experience. Fueled by creativity and teamwork, Team Trydan races ahead with the vision of bringing professional racing excitement within everyone's reach.

Engineering Excellence

We apply classroom knowledge to real-world racing, designing and building our own karts from the ground up.

Competitive Spirit

We compete in regional and national championships, constantly pushing ourselves to improve and innovate.

Team Unity

From engineers to drivers, we work together as one team, supporting each other on and off the track.
