EKVC 2026

The Engine Kart Vehicle Championship (EKVC) is a premier national-level student karting competition hosted by the Coimbatore Society of Racing Minds (CSRM). It brings together engineering teams from across the country to design, manufacture, and compete with high-performance go-karts. The competition focuses on a comprehensive evaluation of engineering capabilities through design validation, cost and manufacturing analysis, and dynamic performance events such as acceleration, skid pad, autocross, and endurance racing. With a strong emphasis on innovation, performance, and reliability, EKVC provides a highly competitive platform for students to apply advanced automotive engineering concepts in real-world conditions while developing essential technical and teamwork skills.
