EKVC 2026

The EKVC (Engineer's Karting Vehicle Challenge) is a national-level student engineering competition organized by the Imperial Society of Innovative Engineers (ISIE). It provides a platform for aspiring engineers to design, fabricate, and validate a go-kart by applying real-world automotive engineering principles. The competition emphasizes both technical knowledge and practical implementation, bridging the gap between classroom learning and industry-level experience.
